To make overnight oatmeal you will need:
- 1 cup of almound milk
- 1/2 cup of oats
- 1/2 cup of fruits (blueberries &/or strawberries)
- 1 Tablespoon of honey
- 1/8 teaspoon of cinnamon powder

Then you place all the ingredients into a jar and close the lid. Place it in the refridgerator overnight.
It will be ready and waiting the next morning as your delicious breakfast!
